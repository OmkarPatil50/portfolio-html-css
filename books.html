Some of the interesting Books, I have Read...!

Raja Shivchhatrapati - Babasaheb Purandare

This book is a creative non-fiction biography of Chh. Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of Maratha Empire. But it is more than that. It is also a cultural landmark

Check it out

India after Gandhi - Ramachandra Guha

The History of the World's Largest Democracy is a book by Indian historian Ramchandra Guha published by HarperCollins in August 2007. A history of the Indian nation after it gained independence from the British Empire on 15 August 1947.

Check it out

Chhava - Shivaji Sawant

Born to Shivaji and his first wife Saibai, Sambhaji was entrusted with carrying on the Maratha empire which Shivaji began. A brilliant tactician, Sambhaji was worthy of the throne of the Marathas, although his rule was short-lived. This book reveals his life story and showcases him for the ruler that he was.

Check it out

Not Just a Civil Servant - Anil Swarup

Not Just A Civil Servant drafts the journey of Anil Swarup, a civil servant who survived despite being politically incorrect.

Check it out
